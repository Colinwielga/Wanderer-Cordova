Skills in this case refers to attributes of you character that can help them overcome obstacles.

Specificity:

Skill can be at various level of specificity from “physically fit” to “I have been training my whole life to kill the man that killed my father” to “climbing”. When the DM comes up with a DC they will select all skills that apply. More specific skills will give you a larger skill bonus than less specific skills.

Rank:

A character can have different levels of proficiency with a skill. To capture this information each skill have a “rank” the higher a skills “rank” the better a character is at that skill.

Helps:

Being “physically fit” makes one better at “climbing”. To capture this sort of relationship on each skill we track what skills it help. We say skill A helps skill B if getting better at A would make you better at B. It is totally possible for two skills to help each other and no limit to the number of skills a skill can help.

Getting started:

Start the game with total skill ranks of 6. Gain a skill rank every session.
